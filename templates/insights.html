Price Distribution

Where the market concentrates.

Relative price-per-sqft index across major Mumbai localities. Worli and Bandra anchor the premium end; Nalasopara and Dombivali serve the affordable segment.
Highest Price Band
₹25K – ₹40K / sqft
South Mumbai and select Bandra micro-markets. Ultra-luxury stock with sea views. Worli, Lower Parel, Prabhadevi dominate this segment.
Largest Segment by Volume
₹8K – ₹16K / sqft
Andheri, Borivali, Powai, Malad, Goregaon. Bulk of Mumbai's residential transactions happen here. Driven by IT worker demand and good connectivity.
Fastest Growing (Affordable)
₹3K – ₹6K / sqft
Dombivali, Nalasopara, Ambernath. Infrastructure investment (Metro, highways) is pushing demand into these areas significantly.
